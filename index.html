Capti is easy to use for children and adults with developmental disabilities, dyslexia, visual and hearing impairment, and other developmental disabilities

  • Language support

    More than 100 languages. Use Capti to find translations and definitions of words and phrases, create and share custom word lists, and improve your vocabulary.

  • Platforms and browsers

    Capti Voice was voted No 1"best text to speech software for Windows, Mac and iOS". Capti will work on Google Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Opera, and Chromebook devices.

  • Formats

    Capti makes it easy and convenient to listen to books on the Internet, listen to news, convert DOC, PDF, Web and ePub files into the speech. Sync your playlist and reading position across all devices.

  • Individual features: vision, dyslexia, etc.

    Extensive support for screen readers such as Jaws, NVDA, VoiceOver. It includes changing the text and changing the color, supports change of fonts, including a custom font Dyslexia.

  • Manage your organization account

    A simple and convenient way to work in a group. Add / remove members in an account of the organization, change roles of members, etc. And also integrated account of students / teachers can be created.

  • Text to speech

    Turn any text into an audio book. Capti will read the text offline aloud. Large selection of voice, accents, pronunciation and speed are provided. Choose a convenient one for you.
